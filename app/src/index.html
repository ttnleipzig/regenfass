regenfass

To install regenfass to your microcontroller, do following steps:

  • Connect the ESP device to your computer using USB or serial-to-USB adapter

  • Select the firmware variant suitable for your device

  • Hit "Install" and select the correct port or find help if no device found

  • Configure your LoRaWAN credentials
